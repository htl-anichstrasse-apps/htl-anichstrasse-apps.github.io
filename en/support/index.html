App Support

This page provides support for all mobile applications published as student or school projects of HTL Anichstraße.

1. About Our Apps

The apps published here are exclusively non-commercial student and school projects of HTL Anichstraße. They are developed as part of the curriculum and serve educational and training purposes. No fees are charged and no commercial purpose is pursued.

2. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • The app won't start or crashes: Please ensure your operating system is up to date. Try uninstalling and reinstalling the app.
  • The app contains a bug or doesn't work as expected: As these are school projects, we cannot guarantee a professional level of support. However, we welcome bug reports.
  • I'd like to suggest a feature: Contact us by email. We'll be happy to forward your feedback to the relevant student teams.

3. Contact

For questions, issues, or feedback about our apps, please contact us by email. Please include the name of the app in your message so we can forward your request to the correct project team.

4. Note on Support Scope

Since the apps are developed by students as part of their curriculum, commercial support (e.g. guaranteed response times or service level agreements) cannot be offered. We do, however, endeavor to process incoming requests promptly.
