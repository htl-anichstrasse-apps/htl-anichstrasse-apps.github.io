2. Data Minimization Principle

Our apps are developed as educational and training projects and adhere to the principle of data minimization (Art. 5 GDPR). Processing is based on our legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) in providing functional educational projects within the scope of our educational mandate.

  • Data minimization: Many of our apps do not collect personal data. If required for a specific feature, only the data strictly necessary for that purpose is processed.
  • No sensitive data: No financial information, biometric data, or other sensitive data is collected or transmitted to third parties.

3. Permissions and Local Data

Our apps only request permissions that are technically required for the respective functionality. Depending on the app type, the following accesses may be used:

  • Location data: Some apps use your device's location to provide location-based information (e.g. local weather data such as temperature and wind speed). This data is processed exclusively to provide the feature within the app. It is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties, unless technically required for retrieving data from external weather APIs.
  • Credentials and authentication: Certain apps require login with username and password for specific features. These credentials are transmitted exclusively via an encrypted connection and serve only for authentication to services operated and hosted within the school network. No storage outside the designated systems or disclosure to third parties takes place.
  • Camera and gallery: Some apps require access to the camera or photo library (e.g. for scanning QR codes or uploading a profile picture). These accesses only occur after explicit approval by the user. Images are, unless otherwise stated, only processed locally or transmitted encrypted to the school's internal systems.
  • Notifications: If the app uses push notifications, these are only activated with your consent. We use this feature exclusively for important app-related information (e.g. weather warnings or status updates). You can revoke this permission at any time in your device's system settings.
  • Storage: To save settings, offline content, or to export data (e.g. log files or PDF reports), the app may request access to local storage. This data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
  • Bluetooth and local networks: For communication with school hardware or sensors, access to Bluetooth or the local network may be required. This connection serves exclusively for technical data exchange between the app and the corresponding device.
  • Device identifiers: For error analysis and improving app stability, anonymized technical identifiers may be processed. No link to your person or school account is made, unless explicitly required for a specific feature.
  • Encryption: To protect the security of your data during transmission, we use state-of-the-art encryption methods (e.g. SSL/TLS via HTTPS).

All permissions can be revoked by the user at any time via the operating system settings (iOS/Android).

4. Processing on School Servers

Some apps may process and store data on the school's IT systems when required for the respective app purpose (e.g. user accounts, synchronization, project-related content, or game and learning progress).

In such cases, the following principles apply:

  • Technical access: Each connection to the server technically transmits your IP address. This is only used for the duration of the communication and not stored permanently.
  • Storage: Processing takes place exclusively on HTL Anichstraße servers, unless otherwise stated in the app.
  • Purpose: Used only for the operation of the respective app.

5. Use of Third-Party Services

Some features of our apps may use services from specialized third-party providers (e.g. for integrating maps, conducting surveys, or providing content) to ensure the functionality and quality of school projects.

  • Anonymized processing: Where data is processed or stored via these services, this is done in anonymized form where possible. No real names, email addresses, or account data are transmitted without your explicit consent. No combination of this data with other information to identify you takes place.
  • Technical necessity: When accessing features that load content from third parties, your IP address is technically transmitted to the respective provider, as they would otherwise be unable to send the data to your device.
  • Service providers: Services used may include platforms from providers such as Google (Ireland Limited), Microsoft (Ireland Operations Limited), or comparable European infrastructure service providers.
  • Purpose: Use is exclusively for the operation, analysis, and continuous improvement of our apps within the scope of our educational mandate.

6. App Platforms (Google and Apple)

When downloading and installing the app, information is transmitted to the respective store operator:

  • Google Play Store: Operated by Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland.
  • Apple App Store: Operated by Apple Inc., One Apple Park Way, Cupertino, California, USA.

We have no influence over data collection by these platforms (e.g. account data, installation time). Please consult the privacy notices of Google and Apple.

7. Your Rights

You have the rights of access, rectification, erasure, and objection under the GDPR. Since many apps operate anonymously, we may need your cooperation to process your request (e.g. providing an anonymous app ID). Contact us with any questions at the email address above.
